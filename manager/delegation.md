# Delegation Model

## Purpose

Specify how Manager assigns tasks to human and AI roles under policy constraints.

## Key Concepts

- Role assignment with explicit authority.
- Delegation contracts include budget and risk bounds.
- Task acceptance, execution, and verification checkpoints.

## Relationship to Polaris Architecture

Delegation operationalizes governance roles and ties into task schemas in `specs/task-schema.md`.
